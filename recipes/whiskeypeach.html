Whiskey Peach

Description

Perfect summertime drink!

Ingredients

  • Â½ cup ice
  • 1 tablespoon peach jam
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 2 fl oz whiskey
  • lemon twist, for garnish
  • SPECIAL EQUIPMENT: cocktail shaker

Steps

  1. In a cocktail shaker, combine the ice, peach jam, lemon juice, and whiskey.

  2. Cover with the lid and shake vigorously for 30 seconds.

  3. Strain into desired serving glass.

  4. Garnish with a lemon twist.

  5. Enjoy!
